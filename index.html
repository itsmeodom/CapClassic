About

The Capital Classic is a team golf tournament in Northern California starting on Friday, March 13 and concluding on Sunday, March 15.

12 players split into 3 teams as evenly as possible by handicap, competing across 3 days.

Games

Day 1: Apple Mountain (Camino)
  • Paired scramble format.
  • Two players hit, pair selects the preferred ball to continue play.
  • Points awarded to the 4 pairs with the best net scores (Grint handicap).
Day 2: The Ridge (Auburn)
  • Paired best ball competition.
  • Lower score of the pair counts each hole.
  • Points awarded to matchup winners based on total net score.
  • Bonus point for best net score of the day.
Day 3: Ancil Hoffman (Sacramento)
  • Individual match play.
  • Point gained per hole won.
  • Up to 3 points per matchup.
  • Bonus points for best net performances.
