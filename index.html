About

The Capital Classic is a team golf tournament in Northern California starting on Friday, March 13 and concluding on Sunday, March 15.

12 players split by handicap into 3 teams for 3 days of team golf competition.

Games

Day 1: Apple Mountain (Camino) — Paired scramble

Two players hit, pair selects the preferred ball to continue play.

  • The pairs with the 4 best net scores (calculated using Grint handicap) will earn points for their team.
Day 2: The Ridge (Auburn) — Paired best ball

2v2 matchup where the lower score of a pair counts toward a team’s total score. Example: if Player A shoots a 3 and Player B shoots a 4, the team records a 3 for that hole.

  • Pairs that win their matchup (calculated automatically in the app using Grint handicaps) earn 3 points for their team.
  • Bonus point to the team with the player with the best net score of the day.
Day 3: Ancil Hoffman (Sacramento) — Individual match play

1v1 matchup where a point is gained for each hole where you score better than your opponent.

  • Points awarded for each matchup won — up to 3 points per matchup by winning a round by at least 3 holes.
  • Bonus point for the player with the best net score of the day.
  • Additional bonus points for the player with the best combined net score over Day 2 and Day 3.
