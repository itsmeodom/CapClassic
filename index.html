Games and Team Scoring

Day 1: Apple Mountain (Camino) — Paired scramble

Two players hit, pair selects the preferred ball to continue play.

  • The pairs with the 4 best net scores (calculated using Grint handicap) will earn points for their team.
  • Minimum 5 tee shots per player
  • Must use at least one tee shot from each player on final four holes (15-18)
Day 2: The Ridge (Auburn) — Paired best ball

2v2 matchup where the lower score of a pair counts toward a team’s total score. Example: if Player A shoots a 3 and Player B shoots a 4, the team records a 3 for that hole.

  • Pairs that win their matchup (calculated automatically in the app using Grint handicaps) earn 3 points for their team.
  • Bonus point to the team with the player with the best net score of the day.
Day 3: Ancil Hoffman (Sacramento) — Individual match play

1v1 matchup where a point is gained for each hole where you score better than your opponent.

  • Points awarded for each matchup won — up to 3 points per matchup by winning a round by at least 3 holes.
  • Bonus point for the player with the best net score of the day.
  • Additional bonus points for the player with the best combined net score over Day 2 and Day 3.

General Rules

  • Record scores using your Grint handicap or another golf app—whichever provides the most accurate handicap.
  • Penalty drops for water hazards, out of bounds, unplayable lies, and re-tees count as one stroke.
  • If a ball goes out of bounds or into the water, drop within two club lengths of where it last crossed out of play.
  • If a drive is shanked, either play the ball where it finishes or re-tee. In either case, the next shot is your third.
  • If a ball comes to rest in a clear divot, sprinkler depression, or similar abnormal course condition, move it slightly to create a reasonable lie without penalty.
  • If a ball lies directly on exposed tree roots that could damage a club or cause injury, move it slightly to create a safe lie without penalty.
  • If a ball lies on a cart path, move it to the nearest point of grass without penalty.
  • Gallery Rule. If a ball appears to have landed in play but cannot be found, take a free drop in the area where it likely landed.
  • No mulligans.
